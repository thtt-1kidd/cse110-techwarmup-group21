# Step 1 Results

To choose 5 websites from the 50 generated ideas, we first narrowed the list down to 11 websites, about one strong candidate selected by each team member. Each team member evaluated their options using the [rubric](../RUBRIC.MD) and selected the website that best matched the criteria.

After that, we came together as a team and reviewed those 11 websites again using the same rubric. From that discussion, we selected 5 websites for the first step of refinement.

## Why We Selected These Websites

We selected these websites because they showed stronger performance in interactivity, visual design, creativity, and usability compared to the others.

### 1. Strong Interactivity

One important reason we selected these websites is that they were more interactive and engaging. For example, some of the candidates included a full game system with token tracking, spinning reels, random outcomes, and result updates. These features made the experience feel more complete and entertaining. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0} :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

### 2. Creative Theme and Humor

We also liked websites that had a clear and original concept. In the examples we reviewed, the AI token casino / slot machine theme was creative and memorable. The websites used satire and humor to make the user experience more interesting, which helped them stand out from more basic designs. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2} :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

### 3. Consistent Visual Design

Another reason we selected these websites is that they used more consistent color schemes and stronger visual identity. One design used a dark blue background with gold and cyan highlights, while another used a neon arcade style. In both cases, the colors were applied consistently across the page, which made the websites look polished and visually appealing. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4} :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

### 4. Feedback and Animation

We preferred websites that gave users clear visual feedback. Some candidates included reel animations, flashing win/loss effects, and changing status messages. These details made the interaction feel more responsive and improved the overall user experience. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6} :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

### 5. Clear Layout and Usability

We also selected websites that had a more organized structure. The stronger candidates separated the page into clear sections such as the header, stats panel, controls, result display, and rules. This made the websites easier to understand and use. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

## Observations

A few important observations we made that helped us decide which websites to shortlist are:

- We liked websites that used visual elements like emojis to make the interface more engaging.
- We preferred websites with stronger feedback, such as animation and dynamic status updates.
- We liked websites with consistent color themes, and in general we preferred blue-based palettes over most other options.
- We favored websites with a clearer theme and more memorable presentation.
