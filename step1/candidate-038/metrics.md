# Inference One - Game Metrics & Design

## Economic Model
- **Initial Capital**: 100 Tokens.
- **Cost per Spin (Inference)**: 5 Tokens.
- **House Edge**: Approximately 30%. 
  - Like most AI startups, the burn rate is real and the path to profitability is narrow.

## Probability Breakdown
- **Jackpot (3 matching symbols)**: 1 in 216 (~0.46%). Payout: 50 Tokens (10x return).
- **Partial Alignment (2 matching symbols)**: ~41.6%. Payout: 5 Tokens (1x return/breakeven).
- **Inference Failure (No matches)**: ~57.9%. Payout: 0 Tokens.

## Satirical Themes
- **Token Billing**: Every click represents the invisible cost of running prompts.
- **Hallucinations**: Losses are framed as the model "lying" or "failing to align."
- **Model Collapse**: The reset button is labeled "Pivot," mocking the tendency of failed AI ventures to rebrand.
- **The "Console"**: Status messages mimic terminal output or cryptic AI error messages from modern providers.

## Technical Implementation
- **Vanilla Stack**: HTML5, CSS3 (Animations/Flexbox), ES6 JavaScript.
- **State**: Managed in-memory; resets on "Pivot" to emphasize the ephemeral nature of startup funding.

## Symbol Glossary
- 🤖 **Model**: The promise of AGI.
- 💸 **Inference Cost**: The reality of your cloud bill.
- 🎰 **Hallucination**: High confidence, zero accuracy.
- 📈 **Hype**: Marketing charts that only go up.
- 💰 **Profit**: A mythical concept in the AI space.
